RentFlex - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy. Simply click on the "Sign Up" button, fill in the required information, and you're all set!

How can I list my room for rent?

To list your room, log in to your account, go to the "My Listings" section, and follow the steps to create a new listing OR you can log in ,fill and submit post room form for listing.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We use encryption and follow best practices to protect your personal information.
